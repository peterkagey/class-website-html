Sum remainder

Let $a_1,a_2,\dots,a_{2018}$ be a strictly increasing sequence of positive integers such that $$a_1+a_2+\cdots+a_{2018}=2018^{2018}.$$ What is the remainder when $a_1^3+a_2^3+\cdots+a_{2018}^3$ is divided by $6$?
