Euler's Totient Theorem

Let $a$ and $m$ be relatively prime integers. Then $$a^{\phi(m)} \equiv 1 \pmod m,$$ where $\phi$ is Euler's totient function, which counts the number of integers $\{1, 2, 3, \dots, m\}$ that divide $m$.
