Do any subset of the following:

  1. [≤4] For each part, give an example of two groups $G_i$ and $H_i$ and a homomorphism $\varphi_i\colon G_i \to H_i$ between them with the following properties.
    1. The homomorphism $\varphi_1\colon G_1 \to H_1$ is an isomorphism.
    2. The homomorphism $\varphi_2\colon G_2 \to H_2$ is neither 1-to-1 nor onto.
    3. The homomorphism $\varphi_3\colon G_3 \to H_3$ is 1-to-1 but not onto.
    4. The homomorphism $\varphi_4\colon G_4 \to H_4$ is not 1-to-1 but is onto.
  2. [≤4] Give four examples of subgroups of $S_5$.
    1. $H_1 \leq S_5$ that is not a normal subgroup.
    2. $H_2 \leq S_5$ such that $H_2 \cong C_5$.
    3. $H_3 \leq S_5$ such that $H_3 \cong C_6$.
    4. $H_4 \leq S_5$ such that $H_4 \cong \mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z}$.
